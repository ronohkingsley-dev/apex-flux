DIRECTIVE 02

OPERATIONS

The XP system drives progression from CAD INTERN to INDUSTRIAL MAGNATE. Every KES logged and every week under budget earns XP. The 7-Day Vortex Cycle resets weekly — your ledger archives and an Efficiency Grade (A–D) is calculated based on budget adherence. Tiers unlock as XP accumulates: Intern → Technician → Plant Engineer → COO → Magnate.

DATA SOVEREIGNTY
